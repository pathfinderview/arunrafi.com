About

I'm an introvert who ran a social-experience startup.

I started paying for AI models three or four years ago, when most people were still calling it a waste of money.

I believe AI will change what it means to be a person. For a lot of people, that change will be a real deterrent — a hard, disorienting break from who they thought they were.

Separately, I believe one person — alone, no team — will be able to earn $10 million a year by 2027.

This is my thought space. Whenever something lands in my head worth chasing, it ends up here.

I posted this on Twitter in 2023, before it was the kind of thing people were saying:

The irony of white collar folks building a world of driverless cars, drones, and robots, only to be replaced out of their own jobs, soon. Exciting times.
