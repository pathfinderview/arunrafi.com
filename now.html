Now

Last updated: March 2026

I'm in Bangalore. The weather is doing that thing where it can't decide between summer and rain, which feels about right for where my head is at too.

I work at Stable Money. But most of my time, the real time, goes into going deep on AI. Still barely afloat but learning. That's the part that keeps me up at night and gets me up in the morning.

I write to make sense of what I'm learning. Some of it ends up here. Most of it doesn't. I write these thoughts down, then wait to see if they age well. Some do. Some teach me something else entirely.

Currently reading Sophie's World. There's something funny about reading a novel about philosophy while trying to figure out your own. It keeps pulling me back to questions I thought I'd moved past.

A question I'm sitting with: If you could only be remembered for one thing you haven't done yet, what would it be?
